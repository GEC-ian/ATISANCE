About ATISA

    ✒ Atisa (982-1054 CE) was a Bengali Buddhist religious leader and master from the Indian subcontinent. He was one of the major figures in the spread of 11th-century Mahayana and Vairayana buddhism in Asia and inspired Buddhist thought from Tibet to Sumatra. Bikrampur, the most probable place for Atisa's birthplace, was the capital of the Pala Empire as it had been of the ancient kingdoms of southeast Bengal. Though the city's exact location is not certain, it presently lies in the Munshiganj District of Bangladesh.

    Atisa had secured education of Buddhism along with Hinduism and become a great religious leader. The name of this Techfest has been named after him because this college is situated on Nalanda's land and Nalanda has been associated with Buddhism. At the same time, Nalanda College of Engineering has also been ranked number One in the state's Engineering Clleges in a very short time And has emerged as a better college in recent years.

Events-2021

Artistics Section

Painting & Sketching

Rules & Regulations Painting & Sketching

  1. Only theme(Covid-19, Social Issues, Lockdown2021) based Painting allowed.
  2. Painting must be on white sheet.
  3. It must not be “Copied Content” from Google or YouTube or any social platform.
  4. Send two photos, one photo of your painting and one photo of the painter holding the painting.
  5. Send a description of what your painting wants to convey in maximum 60 words.
  6. You can use all types of colors: Color pencils, crayons, watercolors, etc. Black & white pencil sketches are also acceptable
  7. The pictures must not be taken using any filters.
  8. Do not Write on your painting the required message must be delivered only by your art not text.
Poster Design

Rules & Regulations Poster Design

  1. Only theme (Covid-19, Social Issues, Lockdown2020-21) based poster allowed.
  2. Not on paper, it must be E-Poster created by “MS Word, PowerPoint, Publisher, Photoshop, AI, CorelDraw” etc.. (not on paper).
  3. It must be original.
  4. You can use images, but recreation is must. You can not paste any image as it is.
  5. Size– A4, Portrait or Landscape.
Photography

Rules & Regulations Photography

  1. Only theme (Covid-19, Social Issues, Lockdown2020-21) based poster allowed.
  2. Pictures should be original. Pictures from internet will lead to disqualification.
  3. Filter should not be used.
  4. Describe your photography in max. 60 words.
  5. Caption should be original.

Poetry & Article

Rules & Regulations

  1. Poetry/Article must be original.
  2. It must be relevant to the theme (Covid-19, Social Issues, Lockdown2020-21).
  3. Only Hindi and English contents are allowed.
  4. One may send more than one entry, but for each entry, registration fee will be charged.
  5. Word limit is 300.
  6. There should be nothing objectionable in the content.
  7. You need to submit a typed write-up.
Slogan

Rules & Regulations

  1. Slogan should be original.
  2. It must be relevant to the theme (Covid-19, Social Issues, Lockdown 2020-21).
  3. Slogan should be in Hindi/English only.
  4. Slogan should not exceed 50 words.
  5. The slogan should not contain anything objectionable.
  6. e slogan should be typed in clearly understandable format.
